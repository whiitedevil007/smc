About the Workshop

This hands-on workshop aims to bridge the gap between theortical knowledge and real-world web development practices. Students will learn how to structure web pages using HTML,style them with CSS, and gain insights into practical industry workflow. The session will focus on applied learning and problem solving in design and development

Workshop Agenda

  • 1. Introduction & Icebreaker- 10 mins

  • 2. Understanding HTML Basic- 20 mins

  • 3. Styling with CSS- 25 mins

  • 4. Building Your First Web Page- 30 mins

  • 5. Q&A and Career Guidance- 15 mins
